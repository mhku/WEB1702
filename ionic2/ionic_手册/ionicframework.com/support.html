How can we help?

From community help to premium support, we’re here to help.

Community Help

The Ionic community is your first stop for questions and advice about the Framework. Welcome to the community!

Community Forum

The Forum is the best place to connect with other Ionic developers, ask questions, or even help out others.

Worldwide Slack

Chat in our Slack channel with other Ionic community members, and occasionally some Ionic core team members.

StackOverflow

For crowdsourced technical questions from expert Ionic devs in our community. Also frequented by the Ionic core team.

Business and Customer Support

Run into a problem? Get in touch, we’re here to help. This requires an Ionic account.

Account and billing

Contact our support team for any issues or problems with your Ionic account or questions about billing.

Report services bug or issue

Find a bug in the Ionic services? Have a novel idea? Let us know! Framework bugs, please use the GitHub link below.

Using Ionic Services

Have questions about what Ionic has to offer or how to use our services? Ask us about it.

More Resources

Looking for more resources? Here’s what we have to offer.

Ionic Framework Resources

Ionic Framework guides, videos, books, tools and everything in between for taking your app further.

GitHub issues

The end all, be all Knowledge Base for learning more about how to build even better Ionic apps.

FAQ

You have frequent questions, we have the answers. Stop by our FAQ and make sure yours are answered.
